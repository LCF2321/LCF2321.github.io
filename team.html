Founder

The founder of Desklamp, Mark Johnson, was a visionary entrepreneur driven by a passion for combining functionality with design excellence. His journey began in the mid-1980s when he recognized a need for better lighting solutions in workspaces. Starting from a modest setup in his garage in San Francisco, Mark devoted himself to crafting high-quality desk lamps that not only illuminated work areas but also added an aesthetic appeal to the surroundings.

Our Story

Desklamp Company, established in 1985 by entrepreneur Mark Johnson, originated from a small garage in San Francisco, dedicated to crafting innovative lighting solutions. Initially specializing in traditional desk lamps, the company swiftly gained recognition for its commitment to quality, design, and functionality. Expanding its product line to incorporate cutting-edge LED technology, Desklamp became a frontrunner in energy-efficient lighting solutions. Over the decades, the brand's dedication to craftsmanship and customer satisfaction propelled its global presence, solidifying its position as a leader in the lighting industry, catering not only to office spaces but also to diverse residential and commercial settings.
